BestBuy Electronics — Sales Analysis 2025

1,000 transactions  |  10 product categories  |  Jan–Dec 2025

Q1 — Revenue by product category

Which department is pulling the most weight?

Audio leads at $119,821 — followed by Tablets ($115,723) and Smart Home ($113,336). These three form a clear top tier. Gaming ($30,486) and Smartphones ($38,061) are the weakest performers.

Q2 — Monthly revenue trend

Seasonal peaks to flag for management

Three clear peaks: May ($92,543), October ($80,770), and December ($82,741). March ($50,958) and September ($50,357) are the slowest months — ideal for targeted promotions or clearance events.

Q3 — Average transaction value by age group & gender

Who spends the most per visit?

Women 55+ average $924 per transaction — your highest-value customer. Men 25–34 follow at $916. Women outspend men in 4 of 5 age groups. Men 55+ ($640) are the most price-conscious.

Q4 — Revenue by day of week

Best and worst days for sales

Friday is strongest ($124,360). Sunday is weakest ($100,688). Launch promotions on Friday. Run loyalty deals on Sunday to lift the slow day.

Best-selling hour of the day

When are customers most active in store?

6 PM is your peak hour at $103,359. The after-work window (5–7 PM) drives the most revenue. Staff up between 4–8 PM and run promotions through that window.
